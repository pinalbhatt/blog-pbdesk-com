F# - a new buzz word in the market. With .Net Framework 4.0 Microsoft is also planning to release F# - a multi-paradigm, strongly typed,  functional programming language.

I have been hearing about F# almost since last one year but now planning to enter into this new paradigm. So with this intention I am starting this blog to share my learnings on F#. I will regularly update this post as I proceed with my learning.

My first step will be to know what exactly this buzz word means by answering questions like What is F#? Why F#? History of F#.
